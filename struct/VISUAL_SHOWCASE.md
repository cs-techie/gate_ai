## 🎓 Design Principles Applied

1. **Hierarchy**: Larger/bolder text for important info
2. **Grouping**: Related items in cards/sections
3. **Color Coding**: Status/priority at a glance
4. **Consistency**: Same patterns throughout
5. **Feedback**: Immediate response to interactions
6. **Accessibility**: High contrast, large touch targets
7. **Responsiveness**: Works on all screen sizes
8. **Performance**: Smooth, 60fps animations
